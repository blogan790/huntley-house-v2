# Huntley House — Project Context

## What We're Building
An ecommerce prototype for Huntley House, a luxury puzzle brand. Starting as a local HTML/CSS/JS prototype, eventually migrating to Shopify. Primary acquisition channel: Meta ads.

## Brand Positioning
Warm, human, lived-in luxury. Not aspirational distance — intimate closeness. Puzzles as a ritual of togetherness, not a novelty. Anti-staged. Anti-glossy. Real moments, real people, real mess.

## Photography & Film Direction

### Photography
- **Observed, not directed. Lived-in, not styled. Time spent, not time arranged.**
- Warm, relaxed, candid — camera as a guest, not a director
- Subjects: people together (intergenerational, mixed ages), games, puzzles, food, tables, living rooms, evenings, magic hour
- Moments that are small but meaningful — hands on a table, pieces scattered, people leaning in
- Natural light preferred: warm and muted, occasional bold color in objects
- Textures matter: paper, wood, fabric, food, skin, puzzle pieces
- **Avoid**: studio lighting, clean product isolation, overly styled/overly clean environments

### Film
- Feels like memory, not advertisement
- Observational, unhurried. Simple, steady shots or gentle handheld movement
- Ambient sound where possible. Casual tone.
- Film references: **Airbnb "Live There"**, **Wim Wenders**, **The Florida Project**
- Avoid: highly polished commercial aesthetics, overly clever visual metaphors, hard contrast, dramatic lighting, anything promotional-feeling

## Rejected Website Proposals (Context for What NOT to Do)

Three proposals were received and rejected. Understanding why informs our design decisions.

| Agency | Price | Why Rejected |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Chloe Leonard Studio** | $6,500 | Template-only Shopify customization. No real design work. Too basic for luxury brand. |
| **Fei Nie** | $12,000 | Aesthetic mismatch (neon lime-green gradients). Proposal copy had errors — mentioned "coffee space" and "fashion market" instead of puzzles. Lacked luxury understanding. |
| **Shoppe Theory** | $8,775–$13,755 | Strongest proposal (proven results: 63% revenue increase, 300% conversion rate increase for other clients). Likely rejected on price or timing. |

**Key takeaway:** The client wants a custom, luxury-positioned design — not template work. They're discerning about aesthetic fit.

## Competitor Market Research

Brands studied: **Eurographics, Galison, le Puzz, White Mountain, Piecework, The New Yorker**

| Brand | Positioning | Design Language |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| Eurographics | Heritage, traditional | Textured finishes, nostalgic |
| Galison | Contemporary | Bold color, modern |
| le Puzz | Trendy minimalist | Yellow/black, graphic |
| Piecework | Lifestyle/premium | Editorial, warm |
| The New Yorker | Cultural/editorial | Illustrated, intellectual |

**Market opportunity for Huntley House:** The luxury puzzle space skews either heritage/traditional OR trendy/minimalist. Huntley House has a distinct lane: warm, human, cinematic — a brand with emotional depth, not just pretty packaging.

**Price benchmarks:** $39.95 is competitive at the premium end. Most luxury puzzle brands range $35–$55.

## Design Decisions Made
- Nav: wordmark-only (`HH_logo_type_cream.svg`), not H mark — matches Shopify draft
- Nav background: Deep Cocoa (not Vintage Blue — too light for logo contrast)
- All display headings: `letter-spacing: -0.015em` (tight, matching Shopify draft)
- Hero overlay: gradient not flat — `rgba(47,27,27,0.22→0.38→0.52)`, image should be readable not killed
- Hero entrance: staggered fadeUp 14px (not 30px), 1.2s cubic-bezier(0.215,0.61,0.355,1), delays 0.2/0.4/0.6/0.8s
- Hero CTA: cream-warm fill (#EFE6D8), cocoa text, font-weight 600 — ghost and orange both rejected
- Impact strip: horizontal snap-scroll on deep cocoa replaces orange 3-col grid
- Box images: use as PRIMARY card image (all portrait 1534x1794); puzzle art reveals on hover
- Marquee: fixed height, natural width — editorial film strip, no cropping
- Ritual section: cream-warm panel (olive panel rejected); CTA is olive fill/cream text
- Hero image: placeholder is Rainy Ramen. Will swap for lifestyle photo from shoot.
- The Edit: lives at the-edit.html, homepage shows 3 featured articles with link
